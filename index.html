Guitarist 2002-2015

I've played guitar since I first moved to Nasvhille in 2002. I played with a band for several years, then went on to play with other artists on the road and in the studio. Since getting married in 2006 I decided to take less of the traveling gigs and spend more time in Nashville. I still will play an occasional gig or spend some time doing studio work. However, most of my playing now is recreational as I have pursued other work.

XTS 2010-2014

Xacttone Solutions (XTS) is a company that manufactures guitar pedals and performs system integration. I began working with XTS stuffing circuit boards to help with the pedal production. I then began to get involved assembling pedal boards and rigs for many artists and Nashville session players, including Keith Urban, Peter Frampton, John Oates, and many more.

Take Shape for Life 2014-current

My wife and I decided to create a home business with the company Take Shape for Life. This is a health coaching business that has an emphasis on weight loss through a ketosis-based eating plan. We have been able to help hundreds of people lose weight and create health in their lives. We continue to work this business and plan to for years to come.
